easter eggs

you may have noticed an 'under construction' sign at the bottom of every page. this refers to the common practice of using 'under construction' gifs to represent the ongoing iteration of early websites (esp geocities), encouraging return visits. ofc i know that all modern pages are updated frequently, but as olia says in the linked article, this gif represents the humanity behind the website. i really like this little way of taking ownership of my work.
